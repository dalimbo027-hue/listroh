Child Safety Standards Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Version 1

Short version: We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation. Any violating content or user is immediately removed and reported.

Listroh ("we", "us", "our") is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. This policy outlines our strict standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and applies to all users of the Listroh platform.

1. Zero tolerance policy

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that involves or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any attempt to endanger or harm a child

2. Enforcement

We actively monitor and enforce this policy. If we detect or receive reports of violations:

  • The content is removed immediately
  • The account is permanently banned
  • Relevant data may be preserved for investigation
  • We report confirmed cases to appropriate authorities as required by law

3. Reporting mechanism

Users can report harmful or inappropriate content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken.

4. Compliance and cooperation

We comply with applicable child safety laws and cooperate with law enforcement agencies. This includes responding to valid legal requests and reporting illegal content where required.

5. User responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that any content they create or share complies with this policy. Any violation may result in immediate account termination without prior notice.
