Delete your account

You can delete your Listroh account at any time using either method below.

⚠️

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

Method 1 — Delete directly in the app

In-app deletion Instant

The fastest way. Your account is deleted immediately.

  1. Open the Listroh app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon in the top left
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm by tapping Delete in the dialog

Method 2 — Request deletion by email

Email request Within 30 days

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we will delete your account within 30 days.

Email dalimbo027@gmail.com
